Data Science Modules

About

Data science modules are short explorations into data science that give students the opportunity to work hands-on with a dataset relevant to their course and receive some instruction on the principles of data analysis, statistics, and computing.

With help from the Data Science module development team, a module can be designed and taught in an existing course from any discipline or field.

If you're interested in a module for your course, please fill out this form!
